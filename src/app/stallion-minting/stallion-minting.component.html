The Wicked Cranium Gods Auction with Sandclock


There is chaos all over Osseous and all hell has broken loose. The Gods had to be summoned but there is a caveat. Someone else also managed to listen to their prayers. WATCH FOR WHAT IS ABOUT TO UNVEIL!

The Gods have come to help various charities across the world. Each of them have been assigned to one charity and will be auctioned off. All funds raised will be donated.

The auction will be hosted in October 2021 and the venue is NiftyGateway for now.

There are four Gods - Earth, Fire, Water, Air.

          


Mercy Corps is a global team of 5,600 humanitarians working to create a world where everyone can prosper. With presence in more than 40 countries, Mercy Corp works alongside people living through conflict, poverty and disaster. This year alone, Mercy Corps reached more than 50 million people all over the world with lifesaving and life-changing assistance.

Funds from the auction of the Wicked Craniums Air God will help ensure that when disasters strike, Mercy Corps teams can be there to help meet survivors with compassion and deliver critically needed supplies, like food, clean water, shelter supplies and cash support.

Twitter: https://twitter.com/mercycorps


Acumen is a nonprofit, social impact venture fund that invests in entrepreneurial solutions to the toughest problems of poverty. Acumen hopes to end energy poverty in the next decade so that everyone can live a life of dignity.

For the 230 million people without energy in Ethiopia, Liberia, Tanzania, Nigeria, Kenya, and Ghana, and the more than 750 million people without access worldwide, energy has transformative potential and funds from the auction of the Wicked Craniums Fire God will help ensure Acumen continue to serve those in need of access to energy.

Twitter: https://twitter.com/Acumen


UNICEF, also known as the United Nations Children's Fund, is a United Nations agency responsible for providing humanitarian and developmental aid to children worldwide. The agency is among the most widespread and recognizable social welfare organizations in the world, with a presence in 192 countries and territories.

UNICEF India works in 17 States to protect and strengthen the resilience of children and their families, and to help them cope with adversity during and after a crisis as well as contribute to their adequate mental health and well-being. Proceeds from the Earth God will be donated to UNICEF India, so they could continue to promote mental health initiatives across India.

Twitter: https://twitter.com/UNICEFIndia
